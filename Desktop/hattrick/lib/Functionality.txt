*The APP Features*

The Home page The home page shall feature 3 main contents: 

“Join league to win N100,000”: This is the quiz category where monthly subscribers gets the chance to play for free and accumulates points over a period of 30 days. At the end of the month, the top 10 scorers wins N100,000 each.                                                                                                                                                                

‘’Play to Win N10,000 Now’’: This is the part where players choose to pay for coin to play for 10,000 reward. Now the deal is, any player to score above 100% of the 10 quiz per session automatically wins instant 10,000. If the player cannot score above 100% in this session across 10 lifeline, the game is over. The player can no longer play until the next quiz is uploaded. 

“Hattrik”: In such rare situation where a  player wins 3 quiz session consecutivly and wins N30,000 in total, he wins a bonus N1,000,000 as Jackpot.
